Fizz Buzz is a classic subsitution game, where players count incromentally, replacing numbers divisible by 3 with 'Fizz' and numbers divisible by 5 with 'Buzz'.

Example: 1, 2, Fizz, 4, Buzz, Fizz, 7, 8, Fizz, Buzz, 11, Fizz, 13, 14, Fizz Buzz, 16
